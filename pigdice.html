Rules

  1. Each roll gets a consecutive bonus roll unless a player rolls a 1 or if he holds his/her turn.
  2. A hold(quiting while ahead,"for a while") forfeits a bonus roll.
  3. If a player rolls a 1,all his points during said rolling round will be negated and the turn passed onto the next player
